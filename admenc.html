Our Services

COMPREHENSIVE MARINE SOLUTIONS

From routine maintenance to emergency repairs, we provide complete electrical and engineering services for all types of vessels.

Electrical Systems

Complete electrical installation, maintenance, and repair for navigation, lighting, and power systems.

  • Power distribution systems

Engine Maintenance

Comprehensive engine diagnostics, repairs, and preventive maintenance for optimal performance.

  • Complete engine overhauls
  • Fuel system service

Installation and maintenance of fire suppression, alarm systems, and safety equipment.
